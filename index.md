**Dr Varun Ojha** is a Senior Lecturer (Associate Professor) in Artificial Intelligence at the School of Computing, **<a href="https://www.ncl.ac.uk/computing/" target="_blank">Newcastle University</a>**. He is Artificial Intelligence Theme Leader and Co-I on EPSRC-funded **<a href="https://edgeaihub.co.uk" target="_blank">National Edge AI Hub</a>**. His core research focuses on Deep Learning and Data Science. In the past, Dr Ojha served as a lecturer in Computer Science at the **<a href="http://www.reading.ac.uk/" target="_blank">University of Reading</a>**, UK and as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the **<a href="https://ethz.ch/en.html" target="_blank">Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (ETH Zurich)</a>**, Zurich, Switzerland. Before this, Dr Ojha was a Marie-Curie Fellow (funded by the European Commission) at the **<a href="https://www.vsb.cz/en" target="_blank">Technical University of Ostrava</a>**, Czech Republic. He received a PhD in computer science from the Technical University of Ostrava, the Czech Republic. In his early career, Dr Ojha received a research fellowship position funded by the Govt of India's Dept of Science and Technology at the **<a href="https://visvabharati.ac.in/index.html" target="_blank">Visva-Bharati University</a>**, India. He is a Senior Member of <a href="https://www.ieee.org/" target="_blank">IEEE</a> and a Member of <a href="https://www.acm.org/" target="_blank">ACM</a>. His research works are available in <a href="{{site.baseurl}}/papers">Publications</a>,  <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bNLfWwgl4J4C&hl=en" target="_blank">Google Scholar</a>, and <a href="https://orcid.org/0000-0002-9256-1192" target="_blank">ORCID</a>.
